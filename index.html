About

I am a Computer Science student at Aberystwyth University, currently on my final year. I spent the previous year on a placement working as a web developer for BBC News in London.

I am a foolish dreamer in love with code, video games and good coffee.

This site was built by hand, from scratch, not only as a way to showcase some of the work I've done, but also as a learning exercise, allowing me to play and experiment, and practice my front-end development skills.

The only external front-end libraries it uses are jQuery and normalize.css. To help me build it, I used the pug template language, Sass to style it, and Babel to transpile ES2015+ to ES5. The source for this site can be found on GitHub.
