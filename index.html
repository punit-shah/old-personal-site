About

I am a Computer Science student at Aberystwyth University, currently on a placement year working as a web developer for BBC News in London.

I am a foolish dreamer in love with code, video games and good coffee.

This site was built by hand, from scratch, not only as a way to showcase some of the work I've done, but also as a learning exercise, allowing me to play and experiment, and practice my front-end development skills. It uses the pug template language, is styled with Sass, and transpiles ES2015+ to ES5 with Babel. The source for this site can be found on Github.
